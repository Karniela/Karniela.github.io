Gyroimpact

We propoesd a design to simulate the charge effect and force feedback in games. The device mainly consists of a flying wheel and a brake pad. When the flying wheel starts to spin, it would create gyroscopic effect and can mimic the feeling of energy storage in the player's hand. If the player fire out the attack, the brake pad would stop the flying wheel and create a force feedback. We are still working on the force curve of the device to create a feedback with large force.

DrivingVibe

In this project, we utilized 16 LRAs in a head band to increase the enjoyment of racing games. The feedback pattern is inertia based. When the players accelerate/decelerate/take turn/drive onto rough terrain, they would feel the feedback applied to their heads.
